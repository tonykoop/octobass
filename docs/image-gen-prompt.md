# Image Generation Prompts

Concept visualization only; not fabrication authority.

## Hero Prompt

Photorealistic studio product photo of a monumental three-string octobass concept, a giant upright bowed bass with a towering wooden body, massive neck, broad bridge, long tailpiece, and a visible mechanical stopping system scaled for human controls. Show hand levers and foot pedals linked by rods, cables, or bell cranks to padded or tangent stops that reach the huge strings, with the player console placed beside the body for access while bowing. Deep varnished wood, dark fingerboard, brass and steel linkage hardware, clean prototype finish, realistic scale cues from a stool and floor stand, neutral studio background, soft directional lighting, 3/4 product-photography angle, approximately 50mm lens, high detail, photorealistic.

## In-Context Prompt

Photorealistic rehearsal-room scene of the giant octobass being played by a standing musician, one hand drawing a large bow across the low strings while the other reaches a lever bank and one foot rests near a pedal control. The enormous body rises above the player, with exposed linkages and remote stops visible but safely routed, and a few chairs or music stands nearby for scale. Natural side light, quiet concert-hall or workshop-rehearsal environment, realistic posture, no staged fantasy machinery.

## Detail Prompt

Photorealistic macro detail of the octobass stop-action mechanism: a padded clamp or tangent stop approaching one oversized wound string, a small bell-crank linkage with adjustable nuts and return spring, and the bridge/string path blurred behind it. Show wear-safe contact material, serviceable fasteners, and clear bowing clearance. Shallow depth of field, high detail, realistic metal and wood textures.

## Negative / Avoid

Avoid text, watermarks, logos, impossible lever geometry, guitar-sized proportions, extra strings beyond the three-string concept, extra limbs, unsafe pinch-point emphasis, and fake measured labels.
